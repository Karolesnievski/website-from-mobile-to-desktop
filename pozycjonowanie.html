About first dynasty of Poland

The House of Piast

The House of Piast was the first historical ruling dynasty of Poland. The first documented Polish monarch was Duke Mieszko I (from 930 to 992).

What was the Piast dynasty in Poland?

Piast Dynasty, first ruling family of Poland. According to a 12th-century legend, when Prince Popiel of Gnesen (now Gniezno) died, in the second half of the 9th century, he was succeeded by Siemowit, the son of the prince's plowman, Piast, thus founding a dynasty that ruled the Polish lands until 1370.

Is there a Polish royal family?

There isn't a single Polish Royal House per se, as the office was elective. Most of the Polish Kings were part of a single family, the Piast dynasty. The last legal Piast, Duchess Karolina of Legnica-Brieg, died in 1707.
